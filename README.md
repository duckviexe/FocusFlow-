# 🌳 FocusFlow - Elegant Productivity Timer

FocusFlow is a beautiful, distraction-free productivity timer designed to help you stay in the zone. Featuring a realistic wooden aesthetic, smooth animations, and data persistence, it's the perfect companion for your deep work sessions.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 Visual Design
- **Realistic Wooden Background** - Detailed wood grain texture with plank effects
- **Elegant Typography** - Beautiful Pacifico cursive heading with multiple complementary fonts
- **Glassmorphism Cards** - Modern transparent timer boxes with blur effects
- **Ambient Particles** - Floating light particles for atmospheric effect
- **Smooth Animations** - Fade-ins, pulses, and hover effects throughout

### ⏱️ Timer Functionality
- **Live Countdown** - Real-time updates showing Days, Hours, Minutes, and Seconds
- **Custom Date & Time Selection** - HTML5 datetime picker for precise targeting
- **Event Title Customization** - Name your countdown event
- **Auto-completion Detection** - Automatic celebration when countdown ends
- **Reset Function** - Clear and restart timer anytime

### 💾 Data Persistence
- **LocalStorage Integration** - Saves your countdown even after closing browser
- **Auto-resume** - Continues counting from where you left off
- **Theme Preference Memory** - Remembers your light/dark mode choice

### 🎉 Special Effects
- **Confetti Animation** - Celebrates countdown completion with falling confetti
- **Pulse Animation** - Seconds box subtly pulses for visual interest
- **Hover Effects** - Interactive card lifting and glowing
- **Theme Toggle** - Switch between dark wood and light wood themes

### 📱 Responsive Design
- **Mobile Optimized** - Perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **Adaptive Layout** - Timer boxes rearrange based on screen size
- **Touch Friendly** - All buttons and inputs work great on touch devices

## 📖 Usage Guide

### Starting a Countdown
1. **Enter Event Title (optional)**
   - Click the input field under "Focus Timer"
   - Type your event name (e.g., "Vacation", "Project Deadline")
2. **Select Date & Time**
   - Click the date/time picker
   - Choose your target date and time
   - *Note: The picker prevents selecting past dates.*
3. **Start Countdown**
   - Click the **START COUNTDOWN** button
   - Watch the timer come alive!

### Resetting the Timer
- Click the **RESET TIMER** button
- Confirms with an alert message
- Clears all data and returns to fresh state

### Theme Switching
- Click the **sun icon** in the top-right corner
- Toggles between dark and light wooden themes
- Your preference is automatically saved
